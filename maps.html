Amuay Wildlife Park is a nature reserve situated 350 km west of the Venezuelan capital consisting of a core area of 72 sq km with varying grassland, hills, and marshes.Amuay can easily accessed by Air,Train or by Road.

 

Travellers can take flights from Europe and America to Simón Bolívar International Airport located in the capital of the country,Caracas.

 

From Caracas there are 3 options Travellers can choose :

 

A flight to Barquisimeto(closest airport near park) which takes around an hour.Once you are here you can board our free pickup shuttle service to wildlife park.

 

The distance between Caracas and Amuay is approx. 350 Kms(4 Hours drive),and this can be covered by road or by train.

 

Trains from Caracas usually take an overnight time to reach Amuay and we would recommened travellers to book trains in advance so that they arrive here early morning and enjoiy our beautiful stay.
