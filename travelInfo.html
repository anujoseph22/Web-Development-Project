Amuay Wildlife Park Travel Information


Established : 1985


Location : Maracay, Venezuela.


Coverage Area : 350-sq-kms


Time to Visit : Throughout the Year.


Rainfall : 1877 mm


Languages : English, Spanish


Temperature : Max: 44.00°C, Min: 18.00°C


Altitude : Latitude 190°21' in the South to the longitude 720°58' in the West.


Climate:


Amuay Wildlife Park experiences moderate climatic conditions. The weather is pleasant during winter months and hot during summer. The temperature rises to maximum of 44.00°C and drops to a minimum of 18.00°C. The park receives monsoon showers from the mid of June till October. The tourists can carry light cotton clothes during the summers and heavy clothes and jackets during the winters.


How to reach Us?


By Air:

The nearest airport is Simón Bolívar International Airport of Maiquetia at a distance of 26 km for domestic air travel. This airport is connected to almost all the major Venezuelan cities with regular flights.


By Train / Metro:

Sam Metro Station is at a distance of 30 km and is the nearest metro rail hub connected to almost all the parts of Venezuela by regular shuttles. Barman Station is the nearest railway station connected to Venezuela.


By Road :
The park is connected to all parts of Maracay and other places in Venezuela with very good road connections. Buses ply on Sunday's and holiday's from Maracay to the Park. Chartered vehicles are available to the park on all days from Maracay.
